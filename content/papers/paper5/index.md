##### Abstract

This paper examines employment vulnerability in the urban labour market in India, analyzing the patterns, determinants, and consequences of vulnerable employment across urban centers. The study identifies key factors that contribute to workers' vulnerability and proposes frameworks for understanding labor market precarity in rapidly urbanizing economies.
