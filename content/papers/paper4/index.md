##### Abstract

This paper examines gender differentials in entrepreneurship using a multi-method approach that combines quantitative analysis with qualitative insights. The study investigates how gender shapes entrepreneurial outcomes, access to resources, and business sustainability in the Indian context, drawing on both large-scale survey data and in-depth interviews with entrepreneurs.
