#### Description

This book examines Gujarat's employment trajectory, analyzing how the state's rapid economic growth coexisted with persistent informality in the labor market. Drawing on extensive data and fieldwork, the study provides insights into the nature of growth-informality dynamics in one of India's most economically dynamic states.
