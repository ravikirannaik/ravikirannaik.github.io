I am an Assistant Professor of Economics at FLAME University, Pune, India.
My research focuses on applied labour economics, intergenerational inequality, and the impact of AI on employment. I have
previously held positions at the Indian Institute for Human Settlements
(Bangalore), the Institute of Rural Management Anand (IRMA), and CHRIST University,
Lavasa-Pune.

When not working, I am an avid reader of nonfiction --- anything from
mathematics and physics to AI and beyond. I love watching world cinema,
am addicted to chess, and love watching tennis, football, and test cricket.
