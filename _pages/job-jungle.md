## Job Jungle: Labour Market Simulation Game

An interactive classroom game where students experience labour markets first-hand — negotiating wages as workers and making hiring decisions as employers.

**Course:** ECON207 Labour Economics (FLAME University)

**Concepts covered:** Labour supply (Ch. 2), labour demand (Ch. 3), market equilibrium and wage determination (Ch. 4).

---

### How It Works

- **Workers** receive skill endowments (Blue = skilled, Pink = unskilled) and negotiate wages with employers across 6 rounds of trading.
- **Employers** hire workers to maximise profits using a production schedule with diminishing marginal product.
- **Upgrades** are available between rounds — workers can invest in upskilling or purchase items from the Kite Shop.
- The **instructor dashboard** controls rounds, monitors real-time activity, and displays aggregate market outcomes.
